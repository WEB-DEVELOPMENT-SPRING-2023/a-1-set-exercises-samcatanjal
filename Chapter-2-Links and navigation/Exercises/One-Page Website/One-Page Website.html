Beauty and the Beast

Plot

Beauty and the Beast tells the story of a smart and headstrong village girl named Belle, who finds a seemingly abandoned castle in search of her missing father. To her surprise, the castle is full of talking furniture and an arrogant prince-turned-beast. An unexpected romance awaits the two.
